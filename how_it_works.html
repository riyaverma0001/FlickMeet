How It Works

Getting Started

  1. Sign Up: Create an account on our website or download the app from your app store.
  2. Download & Install: Install the desktop or mobile app, or access our platform via a web browser.
  3. Log In: Enter your credentials to log in to your account.

Scheduling a Meeting

  1. Access the Scheduler: Navigate to the scheduling tool from your dashboard.
  2. Set Details: Enter the meeting title, date, time, and duration.
  3. Invite Participants: Add email addresses or share the meeting link with your attendees.
  4. Save & Confirm: Review the meeting details and save your schedule.

Joining a Meeting

  1. Open the App or Web Browser: Launch the application or open the web platform.
  2. Enter Meeting ID: Input the meeting ID or click on the invitation link.
  3. Join Meeting: Click on the 'Join' button and wait for the host to admit you.

Using Key Features

Screen Sharing

Share your screen, specific applications, or documents with participants to enhance collaboration.

Breakout Rooms

Divide participants into smaller groups for focused discussions and activities.

Recording

Record your meetings and access playback for future reference or review.
